PWP Milestone 1

Purpose

This project will be a professional portfolio website which will showcase my creativity and efficiency highlighting my expertise and professionalism.

Audience

This site will be designed with a professional audience in mind, and is targeted to individuals within the web development and I.T. community nationwide. Target audience to include: startup founders, hiring managers, recruiters, and peers.

Goal

The main goal of this site is to showcase my work and let others know about myself. Secondary goal for users of the site is to initiate contact with me via the contact form or other associated profile links about employment or collaboration on interesting projects that could impact or inspire growth through the connection.

Persona

Name:Varun Sharma

Age:40

Profession:Varun Sharma is HR Manager at a software company called Interactive Technology looking to hire a full-time, in-house Full stack Web Developer. They are looking for developers for their upcoming projects.

Technology:Mac power user. Uses a Mac Pro running IOS 11.4 and a MacBook Pro laptop at home, and an iPhone 12 with an unlimited 5G data connection.

Attitude and Behaviors:Varun is a huge social media user, and seeks potential candidates through tech and fashion community networking events, LinkedIn, and Twitter.

Frustration and Needs:Varun is having difficult time to find talent with experience and knowledge in short period of time.The project is supposed to start in two months and they are still looking for employee. The new project is very important for the company growth so they need someone experienced who can handle the project.

Goals:To determine whether the person is worthy to move next step of the hiring process visiting their portfolio site.

User StoryAs a recruiter, I need a quick way to see if a candidate has the skill and qualifications to assess further and initiate contact for the Full stack web developer position.
